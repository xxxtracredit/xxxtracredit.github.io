EXTRA CREDIT is a series of instructional books & zines made by Jean Y. Kim.

  1. Citrus Hybrids & Cultivars
  2. How to Read Korean
  3. Box of Stars

How to Read Korean is a guide for English speakers to learn how to make sense of Hangul (한글).

Features mini-pages that flip and combine to create different single-syllable words. Notebook spiral bound; 5x8 inches; RISO printed in red and teal.
