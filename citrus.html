EXTRA CREDIT is a series of instructional books & zines made by Jean Y. Kim.

All Citrus Varieties & Hybrids are descendants of five ancestral fruits: Citron, Mandarin, Papeda, Pomelo, and Kumquat.

Citrus Varieties & Hybrids is a single-sheet zine diagramming relationships between combinations and re-combinations of citrus fruits. Punched holes create connections between fruits across pages. RISO printed in yellow, fluorescent pink and green.
